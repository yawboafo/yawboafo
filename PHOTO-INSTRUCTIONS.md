## üì∏ Add Your Professional Photo

To add your professional photo to the hero section:

1. **Prepare Your Photo:**
   - Use a high-quality professional headshot
   - Recommended dimensions: 800x800px or 1000x1000px (square)
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - File size: Under 500KB for optimal loading

2. **Add the Photo:**
   - Save your photo as `profile-photo.jpg` in the root directory
   - Or rename it to match the filename in `index.html` (currently `profile-photo.jpg`)

3. **Alternative: Use a Different Filename**
   If you want to use a different filename, update line in `index.html`:
   ```html
   <img src="profile-photo.jpg" alt="Yaw Koranteng Boafo - Engineering Manager" />
   ```
   Change `profile-photo.jpg` to your filename, e.g., `yaw-photo.jpg`
